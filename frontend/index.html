How it works

From symptom intake to clinician-verified orientation, in a clean, traceable workflow.

01

Describe your symptoms

Answer a focused set of questions (onset, intensity, context) in a guided flow.

02

Guided interview (Infermedica)

Adaptive follow-up questions create a structured clinical snapshot.

03

MIR-style reasoning support (GPT)

Generates a vignette and differential options to support clinician review.

04

Clinician review + consensus

Clinicians review independently and the system aggregates results into a final orientation.
