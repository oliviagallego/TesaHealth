Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-01  ·  Version: 1.0

TesaHealth is not an emergency service and does not replace a medical consultation. If you think you may have an emergency, call 112.

1. What this project is

TesaHealth is an educational platform that helps users structure symptom information and receive educational guidance. It is not an emergency service and does not replace a medical consultation. If you think you may have an emergency, call 112.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data: email, password (stored hashed), role, and basic contact details.
  • Profile & health-related data: information you provide such as demographics and medical background (e.g., conditions, allergies, medications) and symptom answers.
  • Usage & security data: login events and audit logs used to protect the platform.

3. Why we process your data

  • To create and manage your account and keep it secure (authentication, access controls, audit logs).
  • To provide educational guidance, including AI-assisted summaries/questions and, where applicable, review by verified clinicians to reach a consensus outcome.
  • To send notifications about account verification and case status (only if you enable them).
  • To improve reliability and prevent abuse (security monitoring).

4. Legal bases

  • Explicit consent for processing health-related data for educational guidance.
  • Performance of the service to provide your account features.
  • Legitimate interests for platform security and fraud prevention (balanced against your rights).

5. Sharing & third parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may use trusted providers to operate the platform (e.g., hosting, email delivery, and AI services) strictly for the purposes described above.

  • Email delivery to send verification and security emails.
  • Symptom-check / clinical reasoning provider: Infermedica (if enabled in the platform).
  • AI provider: OpenAI (used to generate educational summaries/questions).

Where possible, we aim to minimise what is sent to third parties. Avoid placing identifying information in free-text medical fields.

6. Retention

We keep your data while your account is active and for a limited period after closure for security and audit purposes. Verification documents (for clinicians/admins) are kept only as long as needed for verification.

7. Your choices & rights

  • You can update your profile data at any time.
  • You can enable/disable optional notifications in your profile settings.
  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us.
  • You can withdraw consent where applicable; withdrawal does not affect past processing.

8. Security

We apply access controls, authentication, and audit logging. When deployed, communication should be protected via HTTPS.

9. Contact

If you have questions or requests about your data, contact: tesahealth.tfg@gmail.com
