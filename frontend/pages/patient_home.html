FAQ

Quick answers for patients.

Is TesaHealth only AI?

No. AI helps structure the information, but the final outcome is reviewed by verified clinicians who reach a consensus.

Is this a medical diagnosis?

No. It is educational guidance. Always consult a licensed professional for diagnosis and treatment. If you suspect an emergency, call 112.

How long does clinician review take?

It depends on clinician availability and case load. You can leave and come back later: your case stays in your history.

Can I download my report?

Yes. Once consensus is completed, you can download a PDF report for your appointment.

Can I find nearby hospitals?

Yes. Use the “Find hospitals” tool to locate hospitals near you in Spain and open directions.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. You can request a deletion code and confirm deletion from your profile settings.
