FAQ

Before you apply.

How long does a case take?

Designed as a micro-review: read a short vignette, select category + urgency, add a brief note if needed.

Do I see other clinicians’ votes?

No. Reviews are blinded until closure to reduce bias and anchoring.

How is the final urgency decided?

The platform uses a conservative aggregation rule so safety is prioritized.

What is required for verification?

Professional evidence (degree and membership proof). Access is granted after admin approval.

Does the platform provide treatments?

No. The scope is orientation and safer next steps, no prescriptions, dosing, or treatment plans.
